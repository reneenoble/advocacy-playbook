# AI Tour Workshops

The AI Tour travels to cities worldwide with in-person, hands-on workshops (~75 min). High impact, but requires a polished sample and ongoing maintenance commitment.

## Overview

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Impact Level** | High |
| **Lead Time** | 3-6 months |
| **Effort Required** | High |
| **Best For** | Global reach, hands-on learning, direct feedback |

## How It Works

1. **Call for content** opens in summer for the following fiscal year
2. Submit your workshop proposal
3. If selected, develop and test your materials
4. Workshop runs at stops throughout the tour season

### What You'll Need

- A working, thoroughly tested sample for attendees to use
- Clear step-by-step workshop instructions
- Someone committed to maintaining the content throughout the tour
- Usually an advocacy partner for delivery

### Good to Know

- Regular sessions are 45 min; workshops get ~75 min
- Proctors help attendees who get stuck
- Instructors gather and share feedback with content owners
- Content must align with the tour's themes

## Works Well With

- **For Beginners Courses** — workshop content can become course material (and vice versa)
- **Reactor Livestreams** — do a remote version for people who couldn't attend
- **Blogs** — write up the workshop experience and learnings

## Tips

- **Pin all dependency versions** — this is critical
- **Test in the actual workshop environment** — not just on your machine
- **Time your content carefully** — 75 minutes goes fast
- **Prepare troubleshooting steps** — things will go wrong for some attendees
