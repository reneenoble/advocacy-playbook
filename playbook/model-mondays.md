# Model Mondays

A weekly livestream for AI/ML product demos. You pre-record your demo, it airs on Monday, then you do a 30-min AMA on Discord on Friday. Low prep, good reach.

## Overview

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Impact Level** | Medium |
| **Lead Time** | 1-2 weeks |
| **Effort Required** | Low-Medium |
| **Best For** | AI/ML product features, new model releases |

## How It Works

1. Work with an advocate to prepare a 10-15 min pre-recorded demo
2. Demo airs during the Monday livestream
3. On Friday, join the [Microsoft Foundry Discord](https://discord.gg/nTYy5BXMWG) for a 30-min AMA

Your content doesn't need to be brand new — you can focus on a specific topic or feature.

## Works Well With

- **Discord** — the Friday AMA happens in Microsoft Foundry Discord
- **Reactor** — Model Mondays is part of the broader Reactor ecosystem
- **Blogs** — write a companion post to go with your demo

## Tips

- Show real use cases, not just feature lists
- Prepare for common questions before the AMA
- Have your links ready — docs, samples, getting-started guides
- Pre-recording means you can get it right — take advantage of that
