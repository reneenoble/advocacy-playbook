# Conference Sponsored Sessions

Sponsored sessions at major developer conferences through Microsoft's sponsorship packages.

## Overview

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Impact Level** | High |
| **Lead Time** | 3-6 months |
| **Effort Required** | Medium-High |
| **Best For** | Community credibility, reaching specific developer communities |

## How It Works

When Microsoft sponsors conferences (PyCon, JSConf, etc.), sponsorship packages often include speaking slots. These are **limited resources** and access varies.

**Who manages sponsorships:**
- **Marketing** – Often handles major conference sponsorships
- **Product groups** – May sponsor events aligned with their products
- **Advocacy** – Sometimes manages sponsorships, but often is brought in to deliver the talks rather than manage the relationship

**Key points:**
- Express interest early when planning begins
- Connect with whoever is managing the sponsorship (marketing, product group, or advocacy)
- Slots are allocated among interested teams

## Other Conference Opportunities

If sponsored slots aren't available:

- **Industry/topic conferences** – Smaller conferences aligned with your product area
- **Regional events** – Local meetups and regional conferences
- **Community partnerships** – User groups and community events

## Content Reuse

Conference content can feed into other channels:
- Samples and demos → polished for conference
- Conference recordings → YouTube content
- Session material → blog posts or course content
