## How These Fit Together

The best results come from stacking channels. Here's the general flow:

1. **Build a solid sample/demo** — make sure it works and is well-documented
2. **Create structured content** around it (course, workshop, or video)
3. **Do a livestream** to walk through it with live Q&A
4. **Write a blog post** that embeds the video and links to the sample
5. **Submit to newsletters** to amplify reach
6. **Maintain the sample** — broken demos hurt more than no demo

We've also written up specific combo strategies:

| Combination | Techniques | Best For |
|-------------|------------|----------|
| [AI Feature Launch](./combo-ai-feature-launch.md) | Model Mondays + Discord AMA + Blog | AI/ML features and model releases |
| [Beginners Course + Livestream + Blog](./combo-beginners-course-livestream-blog.md) | For Beginners Course + Reactor Livestreams + Blogs | Educational content with maximum reuse |
