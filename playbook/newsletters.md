# Newsletters

Low-effort way to amplify content you've already made. Microsoft runs several external newsletters — you submit content, and if it fits the theme, it gets included.

## Overview

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Impact Level** | Medium |
| **Lead Time** | ~1 month |
| **Effort Required** | Low |
| **Best For** | Announcements, new releases, driving traffic |

## Available Newsletters

### Microsoft Source Newsletter
- **Audience:** External developers and technologists
- **Frequency:** Monthly
- **How:** Join the internal DL, watch for calls for content ~1 month before publication

### Microsoft for Startups Newsletter
- Regular editions, startup-focused
- May be more selective based on startup relevance

## How It Works

1. Join the distribution list (April Gittens can help)
2. Watch for calls for content — they come out monthly
3. Check if your content fits the edition's theme
4. Submit — your content must be complete by the deadline
5. Not all submissions are included; depends on fit and space

## Works Well With

- **Blogs** — submit your latest blog post
- **Videos** — link to YouTube content
- **Reactor** — promote upcoming livestreams
- **Samples** — announce new sample repos

## Tips

- Have content ready before the call goes out — don't miss opportunities
- Align with the edition's theme for better chances
- Include clear CTAs — what should readers do?
- Track traffic after publication
