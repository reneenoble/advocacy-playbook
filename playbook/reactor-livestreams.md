# Reactor Livestreams

Live demos with Q&A, broadcast on YouTube and kept as evergreen content. Great for walking through a sample or course with real-time interaction.

## Overview

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Impact Level** | Medium-High |
| **Lead Time** | 2-4 weeks |
| **Effort Required** | Medium |
| **Best For** | Product launches, demos, live Q&A |

## How It Works

1. **Partner with an advocate** to co-host or present
2. **Prepare your demo** — a condensed walkthrough (30-60 min)
3. **Go live** — the advocate helps moderate chat
4. **The recording stays on YouTube** for ongoing discovery

### Format Options

- **You present, advocate moderates** — most common
- **Interview/conversation** — advocate interviews you about your product
- **Live coding** — walk through building something with your tool
- **Workshop** — hands-on follow-along (60-75 min)

## Works Well With

- **Blogs** — write a companion post that embeds the video
- **For Beginners Courses** — use course content as the basis for a livestream series
- **Discord** — promote the stream beforehand
- **Newsletters** — include upcoming streams

## Tips

- **Pin your dependency versions** — broken live demos are painful
- **Have a backup plan** — pre-recorded segments or screenshots if something goes wrong
- **Engage the chat throughout** — don't save all questions for the end
- **Share follow-up links** — sample repo, docs, related content
