## How It Works

**Promoting your content:**
1. Join the relevant Discord community
2. Find the announcements channel (may need permissions — contact Sarah Kaiser)
3. Post about your events, content, or releases
4. Stick around and engage with responses

**Cross-server promotion:** Discord can automatically share announcements between servers, so posts on your product's Discord can appear on the Foundry Discord too. Contact Sarah Kaiser to set this up.

**Friday AMAs:** 30-minute Q&A sessions in the Foundry Discord, paired with Model Mondays (demo Monday, AMA Friday).

## Tips

- Be present, not just promotional — answer questions, help others
- Use announcements sparingly
- Don't just post and disappear — engage with responses
- Plan ahead for Model Mondays Friday AMA sessions

## Works Well With

- **Model Mondays** — Friday AMA happens in Discord
- **Reactor Livestreams** — promote streams beforehand
- **Newsletters** — complement async newsletters with real-time chat
- **Events** — promote AI Tour workshops and conferences
